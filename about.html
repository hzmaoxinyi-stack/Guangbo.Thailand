About Guangbo Industries (Thailand)

Your trusted partner in precision plumbing components

Who We Are

Guangbo Industries (Thailand) Co., Ltd is located in AMATA Industrial Zone, Chonburi, Thailand. It is a professional manufacturer of vacuum breakers, sillcocks, wall hydrants, ground hydrants, yard hydrants, garden irrigation valves, hose connectors, nozzles, plumbing valves & fittings, plumb bobs, hardware accessories...

Guangbo Industries is an integrated company with product development, production, sales, and service. With complete production process of forging, precision machining, assembling, and testing. Our products get good reputation among customers in North America, South America, Europe, Middle East and Asia.

Established in 2023, our factory occupies 18,000 square meters' land, has 12,000 square meters' workshop. We have advanced production equipment and testing equipment, including 30 sets of fully automatic machining equipment, 50 sets of CNC and 1 testing center.

Customer satisfaction is our eternal pursuit, while constantly improving management method and customer service, we welcome customers from all over the world to contact us for mutual benefit!
